# ContextDroid roadmap

## v0.1 alpha

- Prove Android diagnostic preservation and raw recovery.
- Validate conservative Gradle, selected ADB, and bounded Logcat behavior.
- Publish reproducible direct archives plus DEB/RPM after all release gates pass.
- Run a small opt-in Android organization pilot.

## Later alpha work

- Expand legally redistributable real-project and device evidence.
- Improve effective-savings measurement after recoveries and reruns.
- Graduate Cursor only after cross-platform lifecycle evidence.
- Evaluate Homebrew after direct archive installation is stable.
- Reintroduce ContextDroid-specific release automation only after a dry-run design is proven.

## Non-goals

ContextDroid does not aim to rewrite every shell command, replace raw logs, claim universal
superiority over RTK, or equate command-output reduction with complete model-session billing.
