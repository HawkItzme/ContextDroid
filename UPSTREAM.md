# Upstream heritage

ContextDroid is an independently maintained downstream product derived from
[RTK](https://github.com/rtk-ai/rtk).

The ContextDroid v0.1 product branch was created from:

- Upstream release: `v0.43.0`
- Upstream commit: `5a7880d404db8364d602f2ecdc41dd790f64013f`
- Upstream repository: `https://github.com/rtk-ai/rtk.git`
- ContextDroid product branch: `product/contextdroid-v0.1`

The upstream Git history is retained. Future upstream changes are reviewed and
cherry-picked selectively; ContextDroid does not continuously merge RTK's development
branch.

ContextDroid is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by RTK or its maintainers.
RTK names and links are used only to identify the upstream work and migration source.

## Benchmarks and claims

RTK performance or token-reduction claims are not ContextDroid results. ContextDroid
publishes only measurements produced by its own documented benchmark corpus and labels
token counts as estimates unless an exact tokenizer is used.
